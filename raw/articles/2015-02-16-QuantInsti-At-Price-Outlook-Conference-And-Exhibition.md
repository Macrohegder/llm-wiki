QuantInsti At Price Outlook Conference And Exhibition

About Event

The renowned Palm and Lauric Oils Price Outlook Conference & Exhibition (POC) is held annually in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, attracting the participation of international players from the oils and fats industry from over 50 countries.

This flagship conference was organized by Bursa Malaysia Derivatives, which operates the world benchmark crude palm oil futures contract or FCPO. This event was earmarked by global edible oil players as a must-attend annual affair. It facilitated the perfect networking platform for the exchange of ideas amongst delegates who hail from different parts of this world and value chain process.

QuantInsti at POC2015

QuantInsti was invited to provide education on futures and options to the conference attendees so that they can obtain information on how derivatives can help their business. With thisworkshop, more physical traders now have the knowledge to use derivatives for trading and risk management.

Dates- March 2nd - 4th, 2015

Venue- SHANGRI-LA HOTEL, KUALA LUMPUR, Malaysia
