Asset Beta and Market Beta in Python

In the stock market, "beta" measures a stock's volatility relative to the market. Beta is a crucial concept in finance and investment, especially when assessing the risk and return potential of individual assets or portfolios. Asset beta and market beta are two key components of the Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM), a widely used method for estimating the expected return of an asset based on its systematic risk. In this blog, we will explore what asset beta and market beta are, their significance in investment analysis, and how to calculate them using Python.
