I’m a Quantitative Research Analyst by profession, with 5 years of experience in Equity & Derivatives Capital Market. I am an MBA and Engineering graduate working in Mumbai for the past 5 years.

I wanted to keep abreast with the latest trends in Quantitative Finance. Hence, I chose to do the EPAT program. I came across Quantinsti when I was searching for programs that would fulfil my need. Since it is a virtual program it worked out well considering my workload. Most of the classes were over the weekend, which meant that my current work schedule isn't affected.

I loved Ernie Chan's lectures. It was great. My journey with EPAT has been good. I attended it remotely. I liked the portal. It was well designed and had recordings of every lecture and notes of every lecture. This helped me for reference.

How has your career shaped up after the course?

I believe, that Quantitative Finance is a secretive field where people do not share ideas. But, I learnt some good concepts and strategies through the EPAT program.
