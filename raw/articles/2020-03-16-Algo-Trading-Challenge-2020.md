Systematic trading strategies aim to automate the trading decisions making process (and execution) in a structured way. In this process, we start with empirical or theoretical models of the financial markets, and try to come up with a trading strategy that exploits our observations.

Alpaca MarketsandQuantInstibring you theAlgo Trading Challenge 2020. In this competition, you will try to come up with a winning systematic trading strategy for US stocks markets and carry out paper trading using Alpaca onBlueshift.
