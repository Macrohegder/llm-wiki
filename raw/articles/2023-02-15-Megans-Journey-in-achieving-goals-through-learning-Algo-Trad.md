Meet Megan Lester, who enrolled for the free courses on Quantra and this is her experience.

Hello, I am Megan Lester and I am currently a full-time student at the University of Bristol studying for a BSc in Biochemistry in the United Kingdom. I found out about Quantra through Facebook ads. I enrolled in theirlearning track for beginnerscomprising 9 free courses on Algorithmic Trading.

Before this course, I had some basic knowledge of python, however, all my knowledge was very isolated. I knew how to do simple functions or could make little games, but I didn’t know how I could actually apply this to be useful.

I did this course to learn about the applications of python and see what sort of careers this could lead to. It gave me an overview of python and its various applications in different scenarios using its vast library.

In my opinion, Jupyter notebooks are excellent at showing the code and information. I also like how you can easily download them to your device locally.

One big concept that I learned about was the idea of modules and packages, especially Numpy and Pandas.The course was very good at introducing these packages and going through how to import them and the correct syntax to use.

The coding exercises were my favourite part! They meant that I had to actively write out the lines of code which made me remember them.I was comfortable with basic python syntax so I was quite happy with the exercises and was able to implement the next syntax quite easily.

Quantra provided clear instructions and I really liked that when I was stuck, I was given hints, not just the answer.

My initial goal was to learn more about the applications of python, especially in finance, and this course has definitely achieved that.
