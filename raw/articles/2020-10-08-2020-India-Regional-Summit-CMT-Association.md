Replay: Navigating the Unthinkable

Couldn’t attend the 2020 India Virtual Summit? You can still watch the webinar recordings captured from the event featuring all of our speakers, including Atul Suri & David Lundgren, CMT, CFA; Rakesh Jhunjhunwala; and Steve Nison, CMT.

The recordings will be available until November 16thand will be hosted on our Digital Event Guide platform, allowing you to connect with other attendees. Once you purchase the replay package, we will email you with instructions on how to access the platform.

We thought that the 2008 crisis was the worst event ever – until we came to a time when the earth stood still.

In this new environment, technical analysis gives us market risk management tools to protect ourselves from falling knives. Whether you are a beginner or a veteran; whether you are a classical technician or a modern trading system architect; whether you act in the short term or the long, there is something for everyone to learn about how to manage the unknown unknowns. Join us at the CMT India Virtual Summit to find out.
