Hi all, we just had our first Systematic Trader Hangout Session yesterday: May 25th, 2025.
The turnout was fantastic, with 9 unique systematic traders in the session.
I learned a ton from everyone in the session, who were well versed in different systematic trading niches than I am.
My favorite quote from the call:
“There is no correlation between being intelligent and being profitable” - this is so true!
What Are Systematic Trader Hangout Sessions?
These are bi-weekly 1 hour video calls over Google Meet where all subscribers to
Systematic Trading with TradeQuantiX
are invited.
Drop in and say hi, let’s network with like minded systematic traders.
Cameras are optional, so there’s no excuse not to join!
The Goals Of These Sessions:
A time for subscribers to call in and discuss ideas and network with me and other systematic traders.
Serve as a non-formal open forum where we can discuss anything systematic trading related.
A way for people to share things they are working on.
Act as a time to kick back and relax and learn from each other.
What These Sessions Are NOT Intended For:
One-One-One mentoring sessions.
A formal training or presentation.
How Are These Sessions Funded:
While these sessions are free to join, they require time and effort from me to organize and host. If you find value in these sessions and want to support future ones, consider
Buying Me A Coffee
to help sustain the community!
Trust me, that coffee will be put to good use, fueling early mornings to develop more trading systems and write more articles!
How To Join:
I will send out a post a few days ahead of time with the meeting day, time, and link to join.
Those posts with details to join will be in the
Systematic Trader Hangout
section of the
Systematic Trading with TradeQuantiX
newsletter page.
Why You Should Join:
It’s incredibly important (and fun!) to network with other systematic traders, discuss the exciting projects we’re working on, and share ideas.
Maybe someone else’s insights will help get you unstuck or give you that aha moment. You may also make a trading buddy too!
There is no pressure, if you just want to just listen in that is completely okay. Just showing up is a big deal!
I’ll see you there!
- TradeQuantiX
