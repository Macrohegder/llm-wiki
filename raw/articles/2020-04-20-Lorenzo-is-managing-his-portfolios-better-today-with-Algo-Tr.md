Here’s our discussion with Lorenzo!

Hi Lorenzo, tell us about yourself

Hi! I’mLorenzo Fusiniand I’m from Italy. I have a Bachelor's Degree in Industrial Engineering from the University of Pisa. And Kitesurfing is my hobby.

From being a partner in the Restaurant business to have your own Real Estate firm - how did Algorithmic Trading come into your life?

My parents had been running a company in the restaurant and tourism industry. Immediately after my studies, I joined the family business. While I was working there with my father, I started a new business in the Real Estate domain, developing and building small residential and commercial properties.

My first stint with Algo Trading was some years ago when I had the need to handle my income. After a long search, I realized that EPAT was what I needed.

How are you applying the knowledge gained at EPAT professionally?

Machine Learning was my priority when I made the decision to join EPAT. Now I’m still improving my skills in Artificial Intelligence and, in the meanwhile, I’m running myquantitative strategieson Python for my own Portfolio. Python and Machine Learning are great tools to work with, not just for the financial domain, but a lot of aspects in our life.

What features of EPAT did you like?

For me, EPAT was a great experience. I really appreciated the EPATian resource section. It’s really helpful to further explore some topics, involved in Algo and Quantitative Trading.
