System Idea:
Let's introduce the momentum trading system. The baseline version of this momentum system is not special in any way in terms of its rules or implementation. The momentum system we will be working with is a pretty standard way of implementing momentum, no secret sauce or special bells and whistles. With that said, we will eventually make the system special by implementing luck controls. The premise of a standard momentum trading system is to buy the top-performing stocks in a universe, hold them for some period of time, then after the given period of time re-examine the universe and sell any stocks that are lagging in performance and replace them with stronger performers. Essentially, the goal is to periodically update the portfolio such that it’s always exposed to the best-performing stocks.
