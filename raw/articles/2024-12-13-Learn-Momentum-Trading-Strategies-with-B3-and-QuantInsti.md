Momentum Trading: A Key Trading Strategy

Momentum trading, a popular method among traders, focuses on identifying opportunities through market trends, sentiment, and volume analysis. This approach is widely used across various asset classes, including stocks, fixed income, and commodities. By mastering these techniques, traders can gain a competitive edge and navigate markets with greater precision.

The QuantInsti course on B3’s platform bridges theoretical concepts with real-world applications. Key features include:
