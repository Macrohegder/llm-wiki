Rajib Ranjan Borah Invited As A Keynote Speaker At The Annual Finance Summit - IIM Kashipur

The Annual Corporate Summit of IIM Kashipur. Encompassing a series of conclaves in each of the domains such as Finance, Marketing, HR, IT, Operations and Consulting, Coalescence 2017 took place on 9thand 10th September 2017.

Finance Summit,a part of Coalescence 2017, provides a platform for experts from the domain to interact with the academia, bringing to life a vibrant exchange of ideas and knowledge. During the previous chapter of their summit, industry experts brought about a many-hued discussion on the scope and future of “Fintech- Disruption in financial services industry”. Past conclaves witnessed enthusiastic participation of corporate leaders from SEBI, Deutsche Bank, Ernst and Young, NSE, BSE etc.

Rajib Ranjan Borah at IIM Kashipur

Our director, Rajib, was one of the keynote speakers amongst the many prestigious speakers at the Annual Corporate Summit of IIM Kashipur. He spoke aboutthe industry and the regulatory expectations allowing the students to become better market participants as well as bridging the gap between the academic market models and the actual market.

As an esteemed panelist, Rajib's rich experience in the Stock Market and the ensuing discussion with the other esteemed panelists brought an exchange of ideas, frameworks, and realities of the markets taking the students to the edge of their seats.

In conclusion, it was indeed a great opportunity for QuantInsti® to be associated with IIM Kashipur and contributing to the world of algorithmic trading andquantitative financeby enlightening the next generation of traders.
