QuantInsti Is The Knowledge Partner For Finbridge Expo 2015

FINBRIDGE, The Financial Services and Technologies Exhibition was held on March 14-15, 2015 at Bombay Exhibition Centre in Mumbai.

Dates -March 14th - 15th, 2015

Venue -Bombay Exhibition Centre - NESCO, Goregaon (East), Mumbai-400063

Event Overview

The entire gamut of technologies in Electronic Trading, Risk Management, Back Office, Banking and other Financial Markets solutions for Stock Broking, Commodities Trading, High Frequency Trading Software (Algorithmic Trading), were showcased during this 2 days mega event.

QuantInsti was invited to become its Knowledge Partner and also conduct workshops on various topics of algorithmic and high frequency trading to the conference attendees so that they can obtain information on how automated trading can help their business. With this workshop, more physical traders now have the knowledge to use algorithms for trading.
