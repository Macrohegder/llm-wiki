## 原文

EPATians' Share | Private Equity Secondary Markets by Rohit Gupta

Description

Rohit Gupta (Vice President at ARC Capital Singapore - Investment Bank) in this session explains the secondaries market and covers everything from the introduction, sellers, case studies to buyers, factors and all that you would need to know to get started. Rohit is an EPAT Alumni.

About EPATians' Share

Amidst these testing times, 'EPATians' Share' is an initiative where EPATians from across domains have come forward to share their wealth of knowledge with EPATian community by way of series of short sessions of 25 - 40 minutes, in their area of expertise.

Key Takeaways

Introduction to secondaries markets

What are secondaries

Overview of the sellers in the markets (cases of angels, PE’s, family offices, VC’s)

Motivation for the sellers

Live case study of a VC in Singapore

Overview of the buyers in the markets

Motivation for the buyers

VC/PE perspectives: factors that attract secondary investments as compared to the primary ones

Live case study on unicorns (Grab- Singapore, Paytm- India, Face++/Touitiao (China) and the VC/family office investments through a secondary route

Growth of secondary fund and comparative analysis of returns made by primary vs secondary funds

About the Speaker

Rohit Gupta is currently working as a Vice President at ARC Capital Singapore - Investment Bank, headquartered in Shanghai, focusing on both public as well as private markets. In his current role, he is working with top VC’ and PE’s from APAC and advising them on the capital raising using alternative ways of financing and also helping their portfolio companies/existing LP’s for potential exits in the secondary markets.

Previously Rohit has worked at aHedge Fundin Hong Kong where he was managing a portfolio of China/Hong Kong equities. He also has experience in investing in the US fixed income markets during his stint as a Prop trader back in India.

Rohit completed his MBA from CUHK in 2015-16 and during his term at CUHK he was also awarded for the Global Leadership Award among the top 50 B schools at St Gallen University- Switzerland for enhancing the peer to peer learning environment.
