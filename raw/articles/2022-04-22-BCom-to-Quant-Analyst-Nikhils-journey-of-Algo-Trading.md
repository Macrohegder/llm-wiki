Hi Nikhil, tell us about yourself!

Hi! I’mNikhil Channarayapattana Prakashand I’m from Bangalore, India. I’ve done my MBA, I’m a Commerce graduate and have worked at ABB Global as a Financial Analyst.

I consider myself a fitness freak, and my day begins with jogging, and workout. I’m fond of travelling and when I have some free time, I dig into books.
