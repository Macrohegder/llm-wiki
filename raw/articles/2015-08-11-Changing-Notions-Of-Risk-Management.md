Risk management for algorithmic trading systems, case studies of recent failures

Increased proliferation of automated trading infinancial marketshave completely changed the notions about risks – both for risk managers, trading managers, and traders. Professionals now need to understanding system and operation risks in addition to managing market, finance, credit and liquidity risks.

In the presentation, a few major risk oversight issues inalgorithmic tradingwill be discussed.

How did Knight Capital lose $460 Million in 45 minutes?

Why was Deutsche Bank forced to close their Algorithmic Trading desk in Tokyo?

What went wrong at Infinium Capital while trading Crude ETFs and why were they fined $ 850,000?

What mistake caused HanMag Securities of Korea to lose 57 billion Korean Won in a few minutes?

and a few more…
