## Visible Content

> In the stock market, some of the most consistent edges in the market come from doing the exact opposite of what feels comfortable: buying when things look objectively weak. The stock market tends to revert to the mean (in the short term), hence buying on selling pressure.

The setup is rare, but has held up across decades of data. It's simple, rules-based, and slightly uncomfortable to trade.

### Strategy Description (from preview)

- Short-term weakness
- Within a long-term uptrend
- Selling into overbought conditions
