We test the average gain from the close until the next day’s close in the S&P 500, the broadest measure of the stock market, on any day (dividends reinvested).
The equity curve shows the compounded result of 100 000 invested at the close and held until the close the next day. We used the ETF with ticker code SPY to perform the test. The average gain per day is 0.04%.
