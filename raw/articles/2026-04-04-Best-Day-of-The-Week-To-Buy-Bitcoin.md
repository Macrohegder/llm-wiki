What is the best day of the week to Buy Bitcoin?
We analyzed long-term BTC data to find out whether timing your purchases actually makes a difference, and the results are surprisingly consistent.
Here’s what the data reveals 👇
📊 When Bitcoin returns are broken down by weekday, a clear pattern appears:
• Mondays have historically produced the strongest average gains (around +0.5% per day)
• Wednesdays and Fridays also show relatively solid performance
• Sundays and Thursdays tend to lag behind
Put simply:
Buying just before Monday has historically provided a small statistical advantage.
Why Is Monday the Best Day of The Week?
👉 It’s the “Weekend Effect”.
Even though Bitcoin trades 24/7, market behavior still changes throughout the week:
• Trading activity typically slows during weekends
• Liquidity declines
• News and sentiment build while participation is lower
• Institutional and professional traders return early in the week
As activity picks up again, prices often adjust upward.
The result is a subtle — but persistent — pattern in returns.
Important: this does NOT mean Mondays always rise.
Short time periods can look very different. Sometimes other days lead. Markets adapt and evolve.
However, across long datasets, early-week strength keeps reappearing.
So what’s the practical takeaway?
✅ If you invest using weekly DCA, timing may offer a slight edge
✅ Late Sunday or early Monday entries have historically been favorable
✅ The edge is small — but small, repeatable advantages compound over time
There you have it: Monday is the best day of the week to buy Bitcoin. This isn’t a prediction.
It’s a statistical tendency.
And systematic traders don’t chase predictions — they look for probabilities.
Most investors try to forecast the future.
Quant investors focus on the odds.
