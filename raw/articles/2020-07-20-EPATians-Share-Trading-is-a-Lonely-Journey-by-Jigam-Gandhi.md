## 原文

EPATians' Share | Trading is a Lonely Journey by Jigam Gandhi

Description

In this podcast, Jigam Gandhi, an EPAT Alumni shares his experiences and learnings from his trading. He treads on the individuality of a trader, the prerequisites, thought process and various scenarios among other things.

About EPATians' Share

Amidst these testing times, 'EPATians' Share' is an initiative where EPATians from across domains have come forward to share their wealth of knowledge with EPATian community by way of series of short sessions of 25 - 40 minutes, in their area of expertise.

Key Takeaways

Edges of an individual Trader

Prerequisites / Processes of a Trader

Thought process / Strategies Which an individual Trader can execute

Scenarios / Strategies, which an individual Trader can filter out.

About the Speaker

Jigam Gandhi has completed his MBA in Finance from NMIMS University. He is currently working with Arcadia Share and Stockbrokers Pvt. Ltd. since 2009 as a Sr.Trader and has a vast experience of 25 years in Equity Market, of which 16 years inProp Desk.

His expertise ranges from handling accounts, settlement of a stockbroker, handling NSE broking operations, including compliance.
