4th of November 2021, was the fourth day in a row with a higher close than the previous close in the S&P 500. How often does this happen?
Let’s test on the ETF SPY since its inception in 1993: It has happened 100 times, so it’s not rare. Let’s explore if this is a tradeable trading edge.
We test the following trading ideas:
Today is Thursday.
The close is higher than the previous close for the fourth time in a row (all days of the week are thus positive).
If 1 and 2 are true, let’s but the close and sell Friday’s open.
