SPY is very mean revertive and RSI is a mean revertive indicator. Does this mean that RSI works on SPY? In this article, we test an RSI SPY trading strategy. It turns out that our hypothesis is correct: the RSI works well on SPY. Even better, we improve the strategy by adding one extra indicator.
We backtest the following trading rules:
If RSI(2) is less than 15, then enter at the close.
Exit on close if today’s close is higher than yesterday’s high.
