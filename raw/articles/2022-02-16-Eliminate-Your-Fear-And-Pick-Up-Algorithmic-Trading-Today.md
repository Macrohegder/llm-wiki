Why should you eliminate the fear of algorithmic trading?

Let us now find out what are those points depicting the qualities that are actually the strengths for any individual with regard to stepping in algorithmic trading domain.

It is possible that you already possess the qualities for beginning your journey with the contemporary approach to trading but you have not realized it yet.

You must eliminate the fear of algorithmic trading if:

You believe in your capabilities

You are an experienced manual trader

You are not experienced at all and wish to start learning algorithmic trading

You are learning from a professional

You have realistic expectations from algorithmic trading

You are using risk management skills in algorithmic trading

You believe in your capabilities

When you believe in your capabilities after deciding to take the algorithmic trading route along with the hard work, you must beat your fear and go forward. You need to trust that the algorithmic trading route is the way to go about your trading journey before you begin.

Also, you should be ready to take the necessary steps needed for learning and/or for taking the practical steps in the live market.

You are an experienced manual trader

Being an experienced manual trader is one of the indications that you can step into algorithmic trading without a doubt.

Since reading the financial market and the creation of trading strategies is not a problem, a manual trader can learn the other requirements such as programming, backtesting, and machine learning. With due efforts and time, the transition from manual trading to algorithmic trading is not a problem.

You are not experienced at all and wish to start learning algorithmic trading

It is not that only experience in something to do with algorithmic trading or a background in the trading journey that can only help. In case you are not at all experienced, you can still pick up algorithmic trading fromscratch.

For instance, take a look at the journey ofEriz Záratewho, being from a pharmacy background, not only learnt algorithmic trading successfully but also became a quant and started his own company.

You are learning from a professional

Learning from an acknowledged professional always helps give the right start to anything new. And, so is the case with algorithmic trading. A professional can teach you the depth of the subject with a practical approach.

So, when you head to the scenario where you no longer deal with the practice capital but invest the real money you can be sure about your approach. For instance, professional traders such as Dr.Ernest Chanare a part of faculty for courses likeEPAT.

You have realistic expectations from algorithmic trading

Having realistic expectations is also an extremely important point here. For instance, before you pick up algorithmic trading, your expectations should not be unrealistic such as “algorithmic trading will not require timely human intervention”.

Such unrealistic expectations can wreak havoc and if you have a more realistic approach to algorithmic trading, then you must eliminate the fear and step into it.

You are using risk management skills in algorithmic trading

With risk management skills in algorithmic trading, you must eliminate any fear of using this contemporary approach since your trades will be much safer.

In case you have equipped yourself with risk management skills, then you get one of the reasons why algorithmic trading is a safe and more effective approach for you than manual trading.

For instance, risk management practices such as stop-loss can help evade any possible loss due to market fluctuations. If you do not want to trade beyond a particular value, stop loss helps you to exit the trade when the value of the stock reaches a particular figure that you have set.

Conclusion

In your decision making with regard to picking up algorithmic trading, there might be several fearful thoughts stopping you from going forward. Irrespective of the experience, background or any other “so-called hurdle”, it is very much possible to pick algorithmic trading.

All you need is dedication, time and effort to put into the learning process as well as a practical approach.
