It uses something called…


#### First Principles Thinking.


Let me explain real quick:

> First Principles is basically what you do when nothing’s working and you throw away the playbook.


First Principles is basically what you do when nothing’s working and you throw away the playbook.

You stop asking,

“What strategy should I copy?”

And instead ask,

“What does RSI really measure?”“Why do traders react that way?”“What happens when that breaks?”

It’s how physicists think.It’s how good quants think.It’s how I try to think when I'm not overwhelmed by coffee and too many tabs open.

So instead of:
