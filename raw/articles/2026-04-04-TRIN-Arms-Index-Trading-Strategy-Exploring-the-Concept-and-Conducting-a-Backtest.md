The Arms index or short-term trading index (TRIN) is a market sentiment indicator that helps gauge the internal strength or weakness of a market. It compares the number of increasing and decreasing stocks (AD Ratio) to the increasing and decreasing volume (AD Volume). We make a backtest of a TRIN Arms Index trading strategy.
We backtest the following trading rules:
When today’s TRIN reading is above 100, we go long the S&P 500 (SPY) at the close.
