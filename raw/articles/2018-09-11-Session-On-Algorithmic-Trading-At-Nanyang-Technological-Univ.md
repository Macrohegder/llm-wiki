About NTU

Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (NTU Singapore) is a cosmopolitan international and research-intensive public university. In 2018, NTU was placed 12th globally in the Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) World University Rankings and is also home to a number of world-class institutes.
