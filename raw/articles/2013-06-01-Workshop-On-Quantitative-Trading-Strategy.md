Workshop On Quantitative Trading Strategy

On 1st June, 2013, Mr.Nitesh Khandelwaltook a workshop on Quantitative trading strategy. The session exposed participants to various Financial Computing tools including R while they learned to model a quantitative trading strategy. During the session, the speaker used various back testing and optimization techniques and deployed the strategy on an open-sourced trading platform.

The workshop was a great success with more than 50 participants joining us from all over the globe. The participants were primarily from trading, banking, brokering and technology backgrounds.
