Financial Revolution – Sentiment Analysis, Artificial Intelligence And Machine Learning

Financial Revolution – Sentiment Analysis, AI and Machine Learning –Zurich, 30 October 2018

Overview

Find out how innovations inSentiment Analysis,AIandMachine Learningare impacting on and benefiting theFinance Sector;  explore the most essential issues at the intersection ofresearchandpractice, and learn aboutlatest modelsandmethods,demonstrating how AI and Machine Learning methods can be used to generate investment decisions successfully.

Speakers include Arun Verma,Bloomberg;Matthias Uhl,UBS;Anders Bally, Sentifi; Gautam Mitra, OptiRisk & Visiting Professor, UCL; Xiang Yu and Christina Erlwein-Sayers, OptiRisk; Ronald Hochreiter, WU Vienna University of Economics and Business & Academy of Data Science in Finance.

The conference is complemented by a workshop on “Techniques and Strategies for Intraday Trading”, a highly practical hands-on experience presented byRajib Ranjan Borah, CEO, iRage Capital (India)and Humberto Brandão, Federal University of Alfenas (Brazil). This conference has been a great success in London, Singapore, Hong Kong and Bangalore; now it comes to Switzerland.

Why Participate?

Hear from leading subject experts from UK, US, Europe and India/Hong Kong

The programme includes the latest state-of-the-art research, practical applications and case studies

Expect technical and in-depth presentations and discussions; we like to stimulate your brain cells!

Excellent networking opportunities throughout the days with all participants, including presenters, investors and exhibitors.

Topics Covered

Fundamentals and applications of machine learning and deep learning

Pattern classifiers, Natural Language Processing (NLP) and AI applied to data, text, and multi-media

Sentiment scores combined with neo-classical models of finance

Financial analytics underpinned by qualitative and quantitative methods

Predictive and normative analysis applied to finance

Behavioural and cognitive science

The future of AI and its impact on industries
