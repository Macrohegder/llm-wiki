Why, What, and How of Short Selling with Laurent Bernut

Short selling is a critical skill for traders building long/short portfolios. This session explores the mechanics of short selling, risk management techniques, and how to generate high-probability trade ideas.

Speakers

Laurent Bernut– CEO, Alpha Secure CapitalWith 18+ years of experience in hedge funds and proprietary trading, Laurent has specialized in short-selling and quantitative strategies. He was a dedicated short seller at Fidelity Investments, working in the Japanese equities market during one of the longest bear markets in history. His expertise in alternative investment strategies makes him a sought-after consultant for hedge funds and institutional traders.

What We Covered

Why short selling is important

How to identify short opportunities

Risk management in short selling

Portfolio allocation for short trades

Why You Should Watch It

Learn short selling strategies used by hedge funds

Understand how to manage volatility and risk in short positions

Discover high-probability trade ideas

Algorithmic Trading in FX Markets by Dr. Alexis Stenfors

This session explores high-frequency trading (HFT) and algorithmic trading in FX markets, covering unique market conventions, liquidity dimensions, and empirical observations.

Speakers

Dr. Alexis Stenfors – Senior Lecturer, University of Portsmouth & MD, Alexis Stenfors & Co.Dr. Stenfors is a former FX trader at HSBC, Citi, Crédit Agricole, and Merrill Lynch. He spent 15 years in banking, trading foreign exchange and interest rate derivatives. His book,"Barometer of Fear", details one of the greatest banking scandals in history. He is a public speaker and consultant on financial markets, risk management, and compliance.

What We Covered

How FX markets differ from equity markets

Importance of liquidity in FX trading

Key trends in algorithmic FX trading

High-frequency trading strategies in FX

Why You Should Watch It

Understand the unique aspects of FX trading

Learn algorithmic and high-frequency strategies for FX markets
