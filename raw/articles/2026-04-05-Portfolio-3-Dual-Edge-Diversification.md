Portfolio Logic
This combination was designed for
behavioral and structural diversification
.
The
Donchian Breakout
captures extended commodity trends, those long directional moves that can run for weeks or months.
The
MA Envelope Mean Reversion
buys short-term equity pullbacks, profiting when volatility spikes or trend pauses.
When agricultural markets trend
, the Donchian system carries the portfolio.
When equities revert
, the Envelope system steps in.
Both trade independently, each using
ATR-based dynamic sizing
to normalize risk across volatility regimes.
