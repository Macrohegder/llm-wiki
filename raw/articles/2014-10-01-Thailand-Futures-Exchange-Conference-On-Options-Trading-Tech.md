Thailand Futures Exchange Conference On Options Trading Techniques

Date:October 2014

Stock Exchange of Thailand (SET), Thailand Futures Exchange (TFEX), FlexTrade & QuantInsti conducted a conference on Option Trading Techniques in Bangkok, Thailand in October 2014. The conference was conducted by QI co-founders Nitesh Khandelwal and Rajib Ranjan Borah and was attended by over 150 traders and a few HNI retail investors.

About the Event

Aimed at familiarizing traders with the newly growing options segment in Thailand, the conference discussed the of Options Market, Risk Management for Options Trading, and various types of trading strategies that can be implemented through Options.

Automation and Algorithmic Trading - which is growing in popularity in the Thai exchanges Trading in Options Derivatives segment - and is a new but rapidly growing segment within Thailand.
