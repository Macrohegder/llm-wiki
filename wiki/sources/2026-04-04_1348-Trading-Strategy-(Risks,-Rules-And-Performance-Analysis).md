The 13/48 trading strategy is a technical analysis method that uses moving averages to identify potential trading opportunities. The strategy is based on the idea that when the 13-period exponential moving average (EMA) crosses above the 48-period EMA, it is a bullish signal that indicates that the price is likely to rise.
