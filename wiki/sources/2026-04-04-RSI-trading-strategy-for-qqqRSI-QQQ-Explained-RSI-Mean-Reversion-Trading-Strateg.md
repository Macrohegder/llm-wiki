Let’s test the RSI trading strategy by setting the RSI reading to lower than x — thus we have an RSI 2 strategy on Nasdaq and QQQ. The trading rules are like this:
RSI(2) must be lower than 10.
If number one is true, then enter at the close.
Exit at the close when today’s close is higher than yesterday’s high.
