A simple backtest shows that even completely random entry signals can deliver competitive returns over time, simply by riding the broad market’s long‑term upward drift.
This highlights how markets quickly price in information, making consistent outperformance via timing very unlikely and reinforcing the value of diversified, systematic investing.
