The RSI strategy with a 90% win rate is built on the triple RSI strategy, a classic mean-reversion approach that targets pullbacks in long-term uptrends.
This high win rate strategy uses three RSI readings alongside a trend filter, waiting for short-term weakness within a bullish market rather than buying strength.
The concept is straightforward: markets often revert to the mean, especially when the uptrend is well established.
Trading Rules for The Triple RSI Trading Strategy
The 5-day RSI is below 30, and
The 5-day RSI reading is down for the third day in a row, and
The 5-day RSI reading was below 60 three trading days ago, and
The close is higher than the 200-day moving average, and
If 1-4 are true, then buy at the close.
Sell at the close when the 5-day RSI crosses above 50.
