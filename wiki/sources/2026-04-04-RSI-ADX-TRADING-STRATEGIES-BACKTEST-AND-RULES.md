The RSI, ADX, (and IBS). These technical indicators are good for short-term mean-reversion or trend-following strategies. They complement each other. RSI and ADX are good for short-term reversals, and opposite, ADX is a quality indicator for trend-following strategies.
We backtest the following trading rules:
➨ If the IBS is below 0.1, we buy QQQ at the close.
➨ We sell at the close when the IBS is above 0.9.
